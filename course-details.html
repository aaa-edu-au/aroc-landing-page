    Aeronautical Radio Operator Certificate (AROC)

    Master aviation radio communication and boost your aviation career.

    This comprehensive AROC course is designed for aspiring and current aviation professionals who need to operate aeronautical radio equipment in Australian airspace. Learn from industry experts, access premium study materials, and get certified with confidence.

    What You'll Learn
    Radio Communication

    Master aviation radio phraseology and protocols.

    Airspace Management

    Understand airspace classes and navigation procedures.

    Safety Procedures

    Learn emergency protocols and distress signals.

    Assessment Preparation

    Get ready for written and practical exams.

    Who Should Enroll?

    • Commercial drone pilots
    • Private pilots
    • Air traffic controllers
    • Search and rescue personnel
    • Emergency service workers
    • Anyone operating in controlled airspace
