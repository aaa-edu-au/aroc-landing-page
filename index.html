About AROC.au

Welcome to AROC.au – Powered by Ace Aviation

AROC.au is the dedicated aviation communication training arm of Ace Aviation, one of Australia's leading aviation academies. Our mission is simple – to help pilots, drone operators, and aviation professionals communicate clearly, safely, and confidently in the skies.

Who We Are

Ace Aviation is a CASA-recognised aviation training organisation with a proven track record of producing some of the most skilled aviation professionals in Australia. With campuses in all major cities, state-of-the-art simulators, and expert instructors, we have become a trusted name for both manned and unmanned aviation training.

What We Do

Through AROC.au, we deliver industry-leading Aeronautical Radio Operator Certificate (AROC) training. This certificate is essential for:

  • Pilots and student pilots
  • Commercial and BVLOS drone operators
  • Emergency services, mining, and industrial aviation personnel
  • Anyone operating VHF airband radios in controlled airspace
