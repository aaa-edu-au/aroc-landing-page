About

Specializing in Aeronautical Radio Operator Certificate Training

The Aeronautical Radio Operator Certificate Course is designed to provide aviation radiotelephone knowledge, techniques, and qualifications for ground operations and aircrew. This includes helicopter hoist operator aircrew, rescue swimmers, loadmasters, and drone pilots operating in restricted airspace.
